Our Official Jersey

Wear the pride of Pasgoda. Our official jersey represents unity, strength, and the sporting spirit of our club.

Design Philosophy

The striking navy blue base signifies our team's depth and determination, while the bold white abstract patterns embody our dynamic approach to the game. Emblazoned with the Sri Lankan flag and our pristine club crest, this jersey isn't just sportswear—it's a statement.

Club Crest

Features our golden crest with a batsman and the crown representing our royal heritage in local cricket.

Breathable Tech

Crafted with high-quality moisture-wicking fabric to keep our players cool under pressure.

National Pride

The Sri Lankan flag is proudly displayed on the right chest, honoring our national cricketing roots.

Custom Typography

Bold, distressed-style numbering to ensure clarity and standard athletic aesthetics on the field.
